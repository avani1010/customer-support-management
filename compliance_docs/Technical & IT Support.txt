
DEPARTMENT: Technical & IT Support
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The IT Support department handles all technical issues related to hardware, 
software, networks, cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity, and system access. 
This department consolidates what was previously split across "Technical Support" 
and "Product Support" — both are now handled here. A ticket belongs in IT Support 
when the customer or employee is experiencing a technology failure that requires 
diagnosis and resolution: something is broken, inaccessible, performing poorly, 
or behaving unexpectedly, and the cause is technical rather than financial or 
administrative.
 
Note that account access issues caused by a billing lapse should be routed to 
Billing and Payments, not here. IT Support handles access failures caused by 
technical faults (password resets, authentication errors, VPN issues, account 
lockouts due to security policies).

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to IT Support when: the customer or employee reports a technical failure 
in hardware, software, network, or cloud systems; a security incident; a 
product malfunction; or any situation where the root cause is technology 
rather than billing, HR, or a service-wide outage. If the issue is affecting 
the entire organisation or a large region and appears to be infrastructure-level, 
also consider Service Outages and Maintenance.
