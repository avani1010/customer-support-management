# General Inquiry — Routing Policy

Route to General Inquiry for:
- Unclear or vague requests, not sure what I need, general question, don't know who to ask
- Organizational structure questions, who should I contact, which department handles this, company organization
- Multiple topics in one ticket, several different questions, covers multiple departments, complex multi-part inquiry
- Company information requests, about your company, company history, business information
- Policy questions not specific to any department, general company policies, procedural questions, how things work here
- Contact information requests, how to reach specific person or team, looking for contact details, department phone numbers
- Navigating support system, how to submit ticket properly, where to find resources, support process questions
- Misdirected tickets, think I submitted to wrong department, ticket routing assistance, need to redirect my issue
- General clarification needed before routing, need more information to understand request, unclear ticket subject
- First contact from new or potential customer without clear need, exploring your services, general interest, initial inquiry
- No clear action or request stated, just providing information, notification without question, status update without specific need
- Administrative tasks not fitting other categories, general administrative assistance, miscellaneous requests
- Organizational updates or changes, company restructure notification, department change information, process changes
- Multi-department coordination needed, issue spans multiple teams, requires cross-functional response
- Feedback not specific to any system, general suggestions, company-wide improvement ideas, broad feedback
- Request for information without clear category, information gathering, exploratory question, broad topic inquiry
- Legal or compliance questions not technical, contract questions, legal terms clarification, regulatory inquiry
- Procurement or vendor questions from internal staff, supplier inquiry, vendor management, purchasing process
- Facility or office location questions, where is your office, visiting your location, facility information
- Career or employment inquiries, job openings, recruitment questions, hiring process information
- Media or press inquiries, journalist request, public relations, media contact
- Language support routing, need assistance in specific language, translation needed, multilingual support request
- Accessibility accommodations, need support for disability, accessibility features, accommodation request
