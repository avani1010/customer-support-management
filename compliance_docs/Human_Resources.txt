# Human Resources — Routing Policy

Route to Human Resources for:
- Employee onboarding, new hire setup, employee account creation, new employee equipment request
- Employee offboarding, employee departure, deactivate employee account, equipment return from departing employee
- HR payroll portal login for employees, payroll staff access request, employee salary portal credentials, HR system login for payroll administrators
- Benefits enrollment problems, cannot enroll in benefits, health insurance setup, retirement plan access
- Employee information update, change employee details, update personnel records, employee data correction
- HR software issues, HRIS problems, HR portal not working, employee management system errors
- Employee timesheet submission problems, HR time tracking portal issues, staff attendance recording system errors, clocking in system not working for employees
- Performance review system access, cannot access performance portal, review deadline issues
- Training and development system, learning management system problems, cannot access training materials
- Company directory issues, employee directory not working, organizational chart access, contact list problems
- Employee laptop or equipment requests, need new work computer, equipment replacement, hardware provision for staff
- Office application licensing for employees, Microsoft Office activation, Adobe license problems, software licensing for staff
- Employee badge or access card issues, building access, security badge, physical access problems
- Company email setup for employee, new employee email account, email forwarding for employee, distribution list management
- New employee VPN account provisioning, HR onboarding VPN setup request, IT access request for new hire, work from home equipment provisioning for new staff
- Employee policy questions when system-related, accessing employee handbook online, policy portal issues
- Internal communication tools for staff, Slack access, Microsoft Teams setup, employee collaboration tools
- Employee self-service portal issues, HR portal problems, cannot update personal information, employee dashboard not working
- Leave management system, cannot request time off, vacation tracking issues, sick leave system problems
- Expense reporting system for employees, cannot submit expenses, reimbursement portal issues, travel expense system
- Employee certification tracking, professional development records, credential management system
- Workplace accommodation requests system, accessibility tools for employees, adaptive technology setup
- Internal IT requests for HR department staff, HR team computer problems, HR-specific application issues
