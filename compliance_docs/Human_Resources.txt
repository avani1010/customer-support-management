
DEPARTMENT: Human Resources
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Human Resources department handles tickets submitted by employees (or on 
behalf of employees) relating to internal HR processes: onboarding and 
offboarding, benefits enrolment, payroll queries, leave and absence management, 
performance reviews, policy questions, and HR system access issues. Unlike 
Customer Service, which deals with external customers, HR tickets are almost 
exclusively internal in origin. A ticket should be routed here when the subject 
matter is an employment relationship, an HR process, or an internal HR platform.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Human Resources when: the submitter is an employee raising an internal 
matter, the ticket references payroll, leave, onboarding, offboarding, HR 
systems, benefits, or employment contracts. Do not route here if the issue is 
about an IT system that is not HR-specific (route to IT Support instead), or if 
the person is an external customer rather than an employee.
