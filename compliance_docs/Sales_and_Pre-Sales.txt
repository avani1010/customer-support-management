DEPARTMENT: Sales and Pre-Sales
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The Sales and Pre-Sales department handles all inbound enquiries from
prospective customers evaluating the product or service before purchase, as
well as existing customers exploring upgrades, additional licences, or new
product lines. This department's goal is to convert interest into a
transaction and to ensure existing customers are aware of the full value
available to them.

In the training data this queue is characterised by requests for digital
marketing strategies, brand growth guidance, pricing information, service
offering details, and sales documentation updates. Tickets here are typically
enquiring rather than frustrated — the customer is exploring options, not
reporting a broken system.

Key ticket types: Request (information on services, digital strategies,
integration capabilities, pricing), Change (updates to sales or marketing
materials, brand strategy updates), Incident (occasionally, a sales tool
or campaign metric tracking issue), Problem (decline in engagement metrics
or brand visibility attributed to strategic rather than technical causes).

COMMON TAGS IN THIS QUEUE
--------------------------
Feedback, IT, Tech Support, Feature, Documentation, Product, Performance,
Sales, Security, Bug, Guidance, Technical, Marketing, Customer, Strategy,
Growth, Digital, Lead.

REPRESENTATIVE EXAMPLES
-----------------------
  - "Brand Growth Services Inquiry: Could you share comprehensive digital
    marketing strategies that can help improve brand development for our
    agency?" [medium|Request]

  - "Inquiry About Data Analytics Services for Investment Portfolio
    Optimization: I am writing to seek detailed information about your
    company's data analytics services for investment portfolio management." [medium|Request]

  - "SAP ERP Integration Details: Could you provide comprehensive details on
    the integration capabilities of SAP ERP for our Outlook project management?
    This would assist us in planning our purchase decision." [medium|Request]

  - "Elasticsearch Brand Expansion Tactics: I am seeking insights to enhance
    brand expansion using Elasticsearch. Could you provide strategies to
    optimize search and improve customer experience?" [medium|Request]

  - "Strategies for Expanding Digital Brand Influence and Innovating Products:
    Our company requires tailored digital strategies to enhance brand expansion
    and product innovation. Could you provide guidance?" [high|Request]

  - "Marketing Strategy Update: I would like to request an update on the
    marketing strategy for our various products to enhance brand growth
    and sales performance." [high|Change]

  - "Enhance Clarity of Pricing Model Information: Request to update pricing
    model options on the website to improve clarity for customers, which would
    assist them in making purchase decisions." [medium|Change]

  - "Revise Sales Documentation: Updating sales documents can boost client
    interaction and improve conversion rates." [low|Change]

PRIORITY GUIDANCE FOR THIS QUEUE
---------------------------------
HIGH: A deal is closing imminently and the customer is at risk of going to a
competitor; an enterprise prospect requires an urgent quote or contract review;
a high-value brand strategy request with a stated deadline; marketing materials
urgently needed for an active campaign launch.

MEDIUM: An active prospect is evaluating the product and has requested
information; an existing customer is exploring an upgrade; brand strategy or
digital marketing guidance is needed for a current project with a moderate
timeline.

LOW: Early-stage exploratory inquiries with no stated deadline; requests to
update marketing materials with no urgency; general information requests about
service offerings; documentation updates for future reference.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Sales and Pre-Sales when: there is no problem to solve — the customer
is asking about purchasing, upgrading, trialling, evaluating, or improving
their brand/sales strategy. If an existing customer has a billing problem with
their current subscription, route to Billing instead. If they have a technical
problem with a product they already own, route to IT Support.

KEY SIGNALS FOR ROUTING TO SALES AND PRE-SALES
-----------------------------------------------
The following vocabulary strongly indicates a Sales or Pre-Sales ticket:
"brand growth", "digital strategy", "marketing campaign", "pricing", "quote",
"trial", "demo", "upgrade", "before we sign", "contract renewal", "volume
discount", "purchase decision", "free trial", "subscription upgrade",
"sales team", "bulk pricing", "annual plan", "integration capabilities"
(in a pre-purchase context), "service offerings", "expand our brand".
If these terms appear and the customer has no active broken system, route here.
