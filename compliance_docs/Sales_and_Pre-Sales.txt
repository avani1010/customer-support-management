
DEPARTMENT: Sales and Pre-Sales
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Sales and Pre-Sales department handles all inbound enquiries from prospective 
customers who are evaluating the product or service before purchase, as well as 
existing customers exploring upgrades, additional licences, or new product lines. 
This department's goal is to convert interest into a transaction and to ensure 
that existing customers are aware of the full value available to them. Tickets 
here are characterised by the absence of any problem — the customer is not broken 
or blocked, they are exploring options. The tone of these tickets is typically 
enquiring rather than frustrated.
 
REPRESENTATIVE EXAMPLES
-----------------------
  - "Could you provide more information on how the NAS-System improves data analytics investment optimization? I am particularly interested in its features and benefits."
  - "Could you provide more information on how the Rode NT-USB Mini can be integrated into hospital systems for securing medical data?"
  - "Customer support reports that a financial organization is encountering integration problems impacting data analytics functionalities across various products. These issues appear to have begun following recent updates, po..."
  - "Noted unauthorized access attempts on the medical data server. The security protocols might be outdated or misconfigured. We have initiated an update of the antivirus software and a review of the firewall. We require you..."
  - "Could you provide more details on the solutions offered for securing medical data?"
  - "Hello, could you offer insights on effective strategies to enhance brand growth, utilizing Oracle Database 19c and Microsoft Azure?"
 
ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Sales and Pre-Sales when: there is no problem to solve — the customer 
is asking about purchasing, upgrading, trialling, or evaluating. If an existing 
customer has a billing problem with their current subscription, route to Billing 
instead. If they have a technical problem with a product they already own, route 
to IT Support.
 
KEY SIGNALS FOR ROUTING TO SALES AND PRE-SALES
-----------------------------------------------
The following vocabulary strongly indicates a Sales or Pre-Sales ticket:
"pricing", "quote", "enterprise plan", "trial", "demo", "upgrade plan", 
"before we sign", "contract renewal", "volume discount", "licence count",
"procurement", "purchase decision", "free trial", "subscription upgrade",
"sales team", "account executive", "bulk pricing", "annual plan".
If these terms appear and the customer has no active problem, route here.
