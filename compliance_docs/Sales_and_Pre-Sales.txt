# Sales and Pre-Sales — Routing Policy

Route to Sales and Pre-Sales for:
- Product demonstration request, want to see demo, schedule product demo, live walkthrough
- Pricing inquiry before purchase, how much does it cost, quote request, pricing information for potential buyer
- Pre-sales consultation, help me decide if product is right, evaluation assistance, suitability assessment
- Feature comparison for buying decision, compare your product to competitors, which plan should I choose
- Custom solution inquiry, need tailored solution, enterprise custom development, bespoke implementation
- Volume or enterprise pricing, bulk discount, corporate pricing, multi-user license quote
- Free trial request, want to try before buying, trial extension, evaluation period
- Contract negotiation, terms and conditions discussion, service level agreement, legal review before purchase
- Proof of concept request, POC setup, pilot program, evaluation deployment
- Integration compatibility before purchase, will it work with our systems, pre-sales technical assessment
- Onboarding process questions from prospect, what happens after I buy, implementation timeline, setup assistance
- Product roadmap inquiry, future features, upcoming releases, development plans
- Case study request for sales evaluation, customer success stories, reference clients, industry examples
- Vendor assessment questions, security questionnaire, compliance documentation for procurement
- Request for proposal response, RFP submission, bid process, tender requirements
- Partnership opportunity, become a partner, reseller inquiry, channel sales
- Educational or nonprofit pricing, academic discount, special pricing for organizations
- Migration from competitor, switching from another product, competitive displacement, transition support
- Product availability for new purchase, when can I get it, pre-order, product launch date
- Sales territory or regional availability, do you sell in my country, regional restrictions, geographic coverage
- Payment terms for new purchase, payment schedule, invoicing terms, purchase order process
- Marketing agency suitability, solutions for marketing firms, advertising agency tools, campaign management features for prospects
- Financial services product inquiry, investment platform features, trading system capabilities for potential clients
