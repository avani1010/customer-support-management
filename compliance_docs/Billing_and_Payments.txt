DEPARTMENT: Billing and Payments
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The Billing and Payments department handles all matters related to financial
transactions, invoices, subscription charges, refunds, payment failures, and
billing discrepancies. A ticket should be routed here whenever the customer's
core concern is money leaving or not leaving their account, an invoice being
incorrect, a payment method failing, or billing integration details. Note that
if a customer cannot access their account because of a suspected payment issue
(e.g., subscription lapsed, card declined), this is a Billing ticket — not an
IT Support ticket — because the root cause is financial, not technical.

Key ticket types in this queue: Incident (invoice errors, payment failures),
Request (billing documentation, payment method updates, integration cost
details), Problem (recurring billing inconsistencies), and Change (updates to
billing processes or payment configurations).

COMMON TAGS IN THIS QUEUE
--------------------------
Billing, Payment, Invoice, Account, Refund, Discrepancy, Amount, Subscription,
Security (billing system access), Documentation, Product, Feature, Feedback.

REPRESENTATIVE EXAMPLES
-----------------------
  - "Concern Regarding Monthly Invoices: We have noticed a billing discrepancy
    on the user's invoice from the previous month. This issue might be caused
    by a synchronization error in the billing system." [medium|Incident]

  - "Billing Inconsistency on Multi-Product Invoice: I am submitting a report
    regarding a billing inconsistency observed on a recent multi-product invoice.
    The amounts do not match our expected subscription charges." [low|Incident]

  - "Cloud Billing Details: Requiring comprehensive information on integrating
    Google Cloud Platform billing into the SaaS project management tool. Please
    furnish the cost-related details involved in this integration." [high|Request]

  - "Incorrect Invoice Generated This Month: The invoice is inaccurate. The
    amount shown does not match our agreed subscription. I have attempted to
    resolve by refreshing the page and logging in again." [medium|Incident]

  - "Payment Discrepancy: I am contacting you regarding an unexpected payment
    discrepancy in my recent invoice. After reviewing, I noticed the charged
    amount does not match the agreed rate." [high|Incident]

  - "Update Billing Processes Using Data Analytics: Requesting an update on
    the billing processes, particularly the payment methods and the software
    utilized for data analytics and investment optimization." [low|Change]

  - "Request for Clarification on Billing and Payment Procedures: I am writing
    to seek clarification about the billing and payment procedures, including
    accepted payment methods and refund timelines." [medium|Request]

  - "Problem with Subscription Services Billing: Noticing discrepancies in
    billing for subscription services, likely due to multiple logins or
    incorrect payment details." [medium|Problem]

PRIORITY GUIDANCE FOR THIS QUEUE
---------------------------------
HIGH: Confirmed fraud, double charge, or payment failure actively blocking
service; billing integration outage affecting multiple accounts; urgent request
for billing configuration details when a deal is closing imminently.

MEDIUM: Single-account invoice discrepancy where no money has been incorrectly
taken yet; billing documentation requests; subscription billing questions;
recurring but non-urgent billing inconsistencies.

LOW: Informational requests about payment methods; requests to update billing
details for future use; post-resolution invoice review; minor billing process
change requests with no active financial impact.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Billing and Payments when: the customer mentions charges, invoices,
refunds, payment failures, subscription renewals, card declines, double billing,
billing integration costs, or any financial transaction. Also route here when a
customer requests documentation or clarification that is specifically about costs
or payment terms (not general product documentation).

Do NOT route here if the customer's issue is purely about a product being
defective (Returns and Exchanges), a system being unavailable for non-payment
reasons (IT Support or Service Outages), or a general pre-purchase pricing
question with no existing account (Sales and Pre-Sales).
