# Billing and Payments — Routing Policy

Route to Billing and Payments for:
- Incorrect invoice, wrong billing amount, invoice discrepancy, invoice error, billing mistake
- Duplicate charge, charged twice, double billing, multiple charges for same service
- Payment not processed, payment failed, credit card declined, transaction error, payment rejected
- Refund request due to billing error, overcharged, charged for cancelled service, want money back
- Subscription renewal issues, auto-renewal failed, subscription not active, billing cycle questions
- Payment method problems, need to update credit card, change payment method, payment information expired
- Account balance inquiries, outstanding payment, account credit, balance discrepancy, payment history
- Billing cycle confusion, when will I be charged, billing date questions, invoice schedule
- Plan upgrade or downgrade billing, pricing tier change, subscription modification charges
- Late payment fees, penalty charges, interest charges, payment deadline questions
- Invoice not received, missing invoice, need copy of bill, billing statement request
- Disputed charges, unauthorized charge, did not receive service billed, want to contest bill
- Tax questions on invoice, VAT issues, sales tax calculation, tax exemption request
- Payment confirmation not received, need receipt, proof of payment, transaction verification
- Credit note request, adjustment needed, billing correction, account credit due
- Prepayment or advance payment, pay early, bulk payment discount, annual billing option
- Automated payment setup, recurring billing setup, scheduled payments, auto-pay enrollment
- Payment plan request, installment options, payment arrangement due to financial difficulty
- Third-party billing questions, corporate billing, purchase order payment, invoicing to different entity
- Currency conversion charges, international payment fees, foreign transaction questions
- Cancellation and final billing, service terminated but still charged, final invoice questions
- Promotional pricing not applied, discount code not working, special offer missing from bill
