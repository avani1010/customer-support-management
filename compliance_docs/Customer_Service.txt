# Customer Service — Routing Policy

Route to Customer Service for:
- General product inquiries, what products do you offer, tell me about your services, product catalog request
- Service offerings questions, what services are available, service packages, pricing information
- Account setup assistance, how to create account, new customer onboarding, getting started help
- General how-to questions, how do I use this feature, basic usage instructions, navigation help
- Product recommendations, which product is right for me, help me choose, comparison between options
- Account management questions, how to update profile, change account details, manage preferences
- Non-technical questions, general information, company policies, business hours
- Feedback and suggestions, feature request, improvement ideas, customer satisfaction survey
- Complaint about service quality, dissatisfied with experience, poor customer service, escalation request
- Marketing services inquiries, advertising options, promotional campaigns, brand management questions
- Financial services questions, investment products, savings accounts, financial planning assistance
- Product availability, is item in stock, when will product be available, backorder status
- Shipping and delivery questions (non-return), order tracking, delivery time estimate, shipping options
- General agency services, digital marketing services, content creation, campaign planning
- Loyalty program questions, rewards points, membership benefits, customer perks
- Documentation requests for general information, brochures, product catalogs, service agreements
- Multi-channel support requests, prefer phone call, want to schedule meeting, escalate to manager
- Language or accessibility support, need assistance in different language, accessibility accommodations
- Partnership inquiries, become a reseller, business collaboration, affiliate program
- Community or forum support, where to find user community, knowledge base location, FAQ access
- General SaaS platform questions, platform overview, feature list, demo request
- Industry-specific inquiries when not technical, healthcare solutions overview, financial sector services, marketing agency suitability
- Event or webinar registrations, training sessions, customer education programs
