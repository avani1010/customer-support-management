DEPARTMENT: Customer Service
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The Customer Service department is the primary point of contact for general
relationship management, complaints about service quality, requests for
guidance that span multiple departments, and situations where the customer
has had a poor experience and needs reassurance. In the training data this
queue frequently receives tickets that are technically adjacent (e.g., slow
platform performance, analytics tool malfunctions, login issues, data
inconsistencies) but where the customer's framing is a request for support,
advice, or escalation rather than a direct technical fault report.

This department handles the human side of support: dissatisfied customers,
escalations where the customer feels unheard, follow-ups on unresolved
tickets, and broad advisory requests (e.g., "how should I approach X?",
"can you provide guidance on Y?"). It also acts as a catch-all when no
other department clearly owns the ticket.

Key ticket types in this queue: Request (guidance, integration advice,
analytics assistance), Incident (platform problems framed as support
requests), Problem (data inconsistencies, forecast inaccuracies reported
for advisory help), Change (strategy improvement requests).

COMMON TAGS IN THIS QUEUE
--------------------------
Tech Support, IT, Feedback, Documentation, Performance, Feature, Security,
Bug, Guidance, Resolution, Product, Integration, Sales.

REPRESENTATIVE EXAMPLES
-----------------------
  - "Request for Support with PostgreSQL Integration: I would appreciate
    assistance integrating PostgreSQL 13 with data analytics tools for
    investment portfolio optimization." [medium|Request]

  - "Inquiry on Data Analytics Tools: I am reaching out to inquire about
    data analytics tools that can optimize investment strategies. Could you
    provide information on available solutions?" [low|Request]

  - "Problem with Investment Optimization: Noticing inaccuracies in the
    investment optimization results due to outdated algorithms and data
    inconsistencies." [medium|Problem]

  - "Intermittent Access Problems on SaaS Platform: Currently facing sporadic
    connectivity difficulties with the cloud-native SaaS system. The suspected
    reason appears to be linked to orchestration resource constraints." [medium|Incident]

  - "IBM SPSS Support Assistance: Seeking guidance on utilizing IBM SPSS
    Statistics 28 for investment data analysis. Would be grateful for any
    resources or tutorials that could be shared." [medium|Request]

  - "Today's Problem with Data Sync: Faced data synchronization problems during
    the usual update process, likely caused by server overload or software
    conflicts. After restarting, the issue persists." [high|Incident]

  - "Strategies for Growing Digital Brand Presence: I am reaching out to inquire
    about the digital strategies your company employs to boost brand growth and
    improve online visibility." [medium|Request]

  - "Problem Reports: Facing data inconsistencies in investment analytics reports
    caused by software integration problems or incorrect data inputs." [medium|Problem]

PRIORITY GUIDANCE FOR THIS QUEUE
---------------------------------
HIGH: Multiple users or the whole organisation blocked; active data
synchronization failure affecting critical operations; customer explicitly
escalating an unresolved high-impact issue.

MEDIUM: Single user affected by a platform issue framed as a support request;
customer seeking guidance on an ongoing but non-critical problem; request for
integration advice where the customer has a specific active project.

LOW: General informational requests about tools or strategies; early-stage
advisory questions with no active problem; routine follow-up on previously
logged tickets; minor feedback submissions.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Customer Service when: the customer is expressing dissatisfaction with
how they were treated; requesting an escalation; following up on an unresolved
issue without introducing a new specific technical problem; asking for broad
guidance or advice across multiple products; or when no other department clearly
owns the ticket.

Do NOT route here if the issue has a clear technical root cause requiring
diagnosis (IT Support), involves a billing error (Billing and Payments), or is
a direct pre-purchase inquiry (Sales and Pre-Sales).
