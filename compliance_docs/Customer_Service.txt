
DEPARTMENT: Customer Service
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Customer Service department is the primary point of contact for general 
relationship management, complaints about service quality, requests for 
information that span multiple departments, and situations where the customer 
has had a poor experience and needs reassurance. This department does not own 
any specific technical domain — rather, it handles the human side of support: 
dissatisfied customers, escalations where the customer feels unheard, follow-ups 
on unresolved tickets, and feedback submissions. If a ticket primarily expresses 
frustration about how they were treated (rather than a specific technical or 
billing problem), it belongs here.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Customer Service when: the customer is expressing dissatisfaction with 
how they were treated, requesting an escalation, following up on an unresolved 
issue without introducing a new specific technical problem, or when no other 
department clearly owns the ticket. This department acts as a catch-all for 
relationship-focused interactions.
