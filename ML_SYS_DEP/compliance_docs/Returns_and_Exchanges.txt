
DEPARTMENT: Returns and Exchanges
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Returns and Exchanges department manages the physical lifecycle of product 
returns, replacements, and exchanges. This includes initiating return requests, 
tracking the status of items in transit back to the company, processing 
replacements for defective or incorrect items, confirming refund timelines after 
a return has been received, and resolving disputes where a return was submitted 
but acknowledgement has not been received. This department is distinct from 
Billing and Payments: a customer requesting a refund before sending an item back 
goes to Billing; a customer who has already sent an item back and is waiting for 
their refund comes here.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Returns and Exchanges when: the customer has a physical product they 
want to send back, has already sent one back, or is asking about the process 
of doing so. Route to Billing and Payments if the conversation is only about 
a refund amount or payment dispute with no physical product return involved.
