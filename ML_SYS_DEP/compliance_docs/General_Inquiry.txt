
DEPARTMENT: General Inquiry
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The General Inquiry department handles questions and requests that are 
informational in nature and do not require any technical resolution, financial 
action, or complaint handling. These are typically pre-purchase questions, 
questions about company policies, requests for documentation or certification, 
questions about compatibility or system requirements, and queries about office 
hours or contact information. A General Inquiry ticket does not require 
investigation — it requires a knowledgeable and timely answer.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to General Inquiry when: the ticket is a question rather than a problem, 
there is no technical failure described, no financial transaction is involved, 
and the customer does not express dissatisfaction. If the question is clearly 
pre-sales in nature (i.e., about pricing, trials, or purchasing), prefer Sales 
and Pre-Sales instead.
