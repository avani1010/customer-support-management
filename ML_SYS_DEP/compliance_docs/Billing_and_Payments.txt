
DEPARTMENT: Billing and Payments
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Billing and Payments department handles all matters related to financial 
transactions, invoices, subscription charges, refunds, payment failures, and 
billing discrepancies. A ticket should be routed here whenever the customer's 
core concern is money leaving or not leaving their account, an invoice being 
incorrect, or a payment method failing. Note that if a customer cannot access 
their account because of a suspected payment issue (e.g., subscription lapsed, 
card declined), this is a Billing ticket — not an IT Support ticket — because 
the root cause is financial, not technical.

  - "Customer Support team, I am reaching out to highlight a significant problem our company is currently experiencing. We are having difficulty analyzing data efficiently due to software incompatibilities. Despite multiple a..."
 
ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Billing and Payments when: the customer mentions charges, invoices, 
refunds, payment failures, subscription renewals, credit card declines, double 
billing, or any financial transaction. Do not route here if the customer's issue 
is purely about a product being defective (Returns and Exchanges) or a system 
being unavailable for non-payment reasons (IT Support or Service Outages).
