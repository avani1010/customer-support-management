
DEPARTMENT: Service Outages and Maintenance
ROUTING SCOPE AND PRIORITY GUIDANCE
=====================================
 
OVERVIEW
--------
The Service Outages and Maintenance department handles incidents and 
communications related to infrastructure-level service disruptions that affect 
multiple customers or a significant portion of users simultaneously, as well as 
planned maintenance windows that may impact availability. Unlike IT Support — 
which handles technical problems affecting individual users or teams — this 
department deals with systemic issues: the platform is down for everyone, a 
region is experiencing degraded performance, or a scheduled maintenance window 
needs to be communicated. A ticket belongs here when the scope of impact goes 
beyond a single user and the likely cause is infrastructure or platform-level.

ROUTING DECISION GUIDANCE
--------------------------
Route to Service Outages and Maintenance when: multiple users are affected by 
the same issue simultaneously, the issue appears to be platform or 
infrastructure-level rather than user-specific, or the ticket is about a 
planned maintenance event. If only one user is affected and the cause is 
likely user-specific (e.g., their VPN config, their device), route to IT Support 
instead.
